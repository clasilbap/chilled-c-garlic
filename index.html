A refreshing and crisp starter, ready in just a few minutes!

Ingredients:

  • 1 cucumber, thinly sliced into strips
  • 3 tablespoons toasted sesame oil (or olive oil)
  • 1 garlic clove, finely chopped
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons apple cider vinegar
  • Toasted sesame seeds (to taste)

Instructions:

  1. Slice the cucumber into thin strips and place in a bowl.
  2. In a pan, heat the sesame oil and sautÃ© the garlic until golden.
  3. Immediately pour the hot garlic oil over the cucumber.
  4. Add soy sauce, apple cider vinegar, and sprinkle with sesame seeds.
  5. Toss everything well to combine and coat evenly.
  6. Chill in the refrigerator and serve cold. It lasts for three days when stored in an airtight container in the refrigerator.
